BOX Breathing

Steps for BOX Breathing

1. Find a Comfortable Position: Find a comfortable seated position with your back straight.
2. Inhale: Breathe in through your nose for a count of 4.
4. Pause: Hold: Hold your breath for a count of 4.
5. Exhale: Exhale slowly through your mouth for a count of 4.
6. Pause: Hold your breath for a count of 4.
7. Repeat: Continue this process for 5 to 10 minutes, focusing on maintaining a steady rhythm.
