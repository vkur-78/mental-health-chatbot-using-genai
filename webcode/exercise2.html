Diaphragmatic Breathing (Belly Breathing)

Steps for Diaphragmatic Breathing

1. Find a Comfortable Position: Sit or lie down in a comfortable position. You may want to place a pillow under your head if lying down.
2. Hand Placement: Place one hand on your chest and the other on your abdomen.
3. Inhale: Breathe in slowly through your nose, allowing your diaphragm to expand. Your abdomen should rise while your chest remains relatively still.
4. Pause (Optional): Hold your breath for a moment if you feel comfortable.
5. Exhale: Slowly exhale through your mouth, letting your abdomen fall. Focus on releasing all the air from your lungs.
6. Repeat: Continue this process for 5 to 10 minutes, focusing on the rise and fall of your abdomen.
