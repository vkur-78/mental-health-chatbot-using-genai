4-7-8 Breathing

Steps for 4-7-8 Breathing

1.Start Position: Begin on your hands and knees in a tabletop position, with your wrists aligned under your shoulders and your knees under your hips.
2.Inhale: Close your eyes and inhale quietly through your nose for a count of 4.
3. Hold: Hold your breath for a count of 7.
4.Exhale Exhale completely through your mouth, making a whooshing sound, for a count of 8.
5. Repeat: This cycle is one breath. Start with 4 cycles and gradually increase to 8 cycles as you become more comfortable.
