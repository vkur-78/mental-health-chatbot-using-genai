Alternate Nostril Breathing (Nadi Shodhana)

Steps for Alternate Nostril Breathing

1. Find a Comfortable Position: Sit in a comfortable position with your spine straight.
2. Hand Position: Use your right thumb to close your right nostril
4. Inhale Left: Inhale deeply and slowly through your left nostril.
5.Close Left Nostril: At the peak of your inhalation, close the left nostril with your ring finger.
6.Exhale Right: Open your right nostril and exhale slowly through it.
7. Inhale Right: Inhale deeply through the right nostril.
8. Close Right Nostril: Close the right nostril with your thumb at the peak of inhalation.
9. Exhale Left: Open your left nostril and exhale slowly through it.
10. Repeat: Continue this process for 5 to 10 minutes, focusing on your breath and the balance it creates.
